This paper presents FIRRE, a two-stage feature reduction framework designed to enhance artificial neural network performance in civil engineering applications. By systematically reducing input dimensionality and improving the condition number of the feature space, the proposed method achieves more accurate and stable predictions while maintaining computational efficiency.
